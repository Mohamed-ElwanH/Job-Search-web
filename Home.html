Welcome to Linked Out

Linked Out is a job search platform that connects job seekers with employers. Whether you're looking for your first job or your next career move, we've got you covered.

What you can do here:

  • Search for jobs by title, keyword, or location
  • Apply for jobs directly through the platform
  • Track all your applications in one place

How it works:

  1. Search for a job using the search bar above
  2. Browse the results and find one that fits you
  3. Click apply and submit your application
  4. Track your application status under My Applications
